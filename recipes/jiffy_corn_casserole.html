Jiffy Corn Casserole

What is The Jimmy Corn Casserole?

Long before the age of man, the Pontiff of Casserole proclaimed himself lord of the Sovory realms. Fearing internal threats, he secluded himself deep within the cheese forest, bastioned in the dreaded "Castle Lactose." Ages passed in silence, until the faithful day. The day where he would finally pass alone the sacred recipe; Jimmy Corn Casserole.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1/2 Large green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (4 ounce) jar chopped pimento peppers
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 (8ounce) package jiffy corn muffin mix
  • 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can whole kernel corn, with liquid
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can creamed corn
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper, and pimento peppers and saute until tender, about 5 minutes.
  3. Combine eggs, corn muffin mix, green chiles, whole corn, and creamed corn in a large bowl with the onion mixture.
  4. Stir together and pour into the prepared baking pan.
  5. Drop sour cream by the spoonful on top of the mixture; cover with Cheddar cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is firm, about 45 minutes.
